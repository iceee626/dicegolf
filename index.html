GAME MODES
🏌️
SINGLE ROUND
A classic 18-hole round of golf. Your score is recorded against par. A straightforward way to enjoy a full round without committing to a longer event.
🏆
TOURNAMENT
Compete across 4 rounds on the same course in a full-length golf event. Every stroke counts, and the lowest cumulative score at the end takes the title.
🌎
PRO TOUR
A season-long competitive format with multiple events, rankings, and progression. Face tougher fields and climb the leaderboard.
⚙️
CUSTOM GAME
Build your own setup. Mix and match number of rounds (1, 2, 3, or 4), holes (18, Front 9, Back 9), and difficulty (Easy / Medium / Hard). Ideal if you want full control over your experience.
🎯 DIFFICULTY
EASY
Generous grids with more fairway and green cells, plus fewer hazards overall. Great for learning the game and achieving the lowest scores.
MEDIUM
Balanced grids with a healthy mix of fairway, rough, sand, and water. This is the standard challenge, offering a fair test without being overly punishing.
HARD
Tight grids packed with hazards and OB cells. Every roll matters, and smart Wildcard use can be the difference between surviving a hole and dropping shots. Best suited for experienced players.
⛳ COURSES
🌲
LITTLE PINES GC
A friendly woodland course and the easiest place to begin. Tree-lined holes shape many tee shots, but the layout is generally forgiving. It still has a few demanding holes that can catch you out.
🌊
PACIFIC BEACH
A coastal course shaped by ocean air, open sightlines, and plenty of sand. The challenge comes from exposed holes, firm landing areas, and bunkers that punish careless misses.
🐊
GATOR CREEK
Based in Florida, where water shapes the round from the opening tee shot through the final stretch. Tight fairways, risky carries, and a demanding island-green finish keep every round challenging.
🌺
SEPTEMBRA NATIONAL
A classic championship-style course inspired by the famous layouts of Georgia. Many holes demand thoughtful positioning, with tricky approaches and demanding greens that put a premium on control and nerve.
VERSUS MODE
Versus Mode lets two players compete head-to-head on the same device, taking turns hole by hole. Both players play on the same grid layouts for a fair match. Choose your names, icons, and game options, then tee off!
🏌️ HOW IT WORKS
🔄
TURN-BASED PLAY
Players alternate by hole. Player 1 plays the full hole first, then Player 2 plays the same hole on the same grid layout.
🎲
SHARED GRIDS
Both players face identical grid layouts for fair competition — same zones, same challenge. Only your dice rolls differ.
🃏
WILDCARDS
Each player has their own wildcard hand. Roll doubles to earn a wildcard for yourself. Use them strategically to gain an edge. Wildcards can be turned off in Settings.
GAME FORMATS
🏆
STROKE PLAY
The classic format. Count every stroke across all selected holes and rounds. The lowest total score wins. A single bad hole won't end the match, keep fighting back!
⚔️
MATCH PLAY
Quadruple bogey on a hole will not end your chances. Each hole is its own contest: the lower score wins the hole, and the lead is shown as ‘X Up.’ The match ends when one side is ahead by more holes than remain. One round only.
📋 MATCH SETTINGS
🥊
ROUNDS
In Stroke Play, choose between 1 and 4 rounds. More rounds mean a longer competition and a bigger test of consistency.
⛳
HOLE SELECTION
Choose 18 holes, the Front 9 (holes 1–9), or the Back 9 (holes 10–18) on a selected course. Then? Tee off!
⚙️
DIFFICULTY
Easy, Medium, and Hard change hazard distribution equally for both players. Higher difficulty means tighter, more punishing grids.
